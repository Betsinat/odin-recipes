Soup

Soup is a warm, comforting dish made by simmering vegetables, meats, or legumes in broth or water. It can be hearty or light, creamy or clear, and is perfect as an appetizer or a main course. Soups are versatile, nutritious, and can be customized with herbs, spices, and various ingredients to suit any taste.

Ingredient

  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil or butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional herbs: parsley, thyme, or bay leaf

Steps

  1. Heat oil or butter in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and garlic, and saut√© until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add carrots, celery, and potatoes, and cook for a few minutes while stirring.
  4. Pour in the broth and chopped tomatoes if using.
  5. Add salt, pepper, and optional herbs to taste.
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-30 minutes until vegetables are tender.
  7. Remove herbs like bay leaf before serving.
  8. Serve hot as a starter or main dish, optionally garnished with fresh parsley.
