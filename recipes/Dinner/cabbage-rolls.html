Cabbage Rolls

Cabbage rolls are hearty and comforting dishes made by wrapping a flavorful filling of ground meat, rice, and seasonings in tender cabbage leaves. They are usually baked or simmered in a tomato-based sauce, making them juicy and delicious. Cabbage rolls are perfect as a main course for lunch or dinner and are enjoyed in many cultures around the world.

Ingredient

  • 8-10 large cabbage leaves
  • 250g ground beef or pork (or mix)
  • 1/2 cup cooked rice
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional herbs: parsley, dill, or thyme

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to boil and blanch the cabbage leaves for 2-3 minutes until soft. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, mix the ground meat, cooked rice, chopped onion, garlic, egg, salt, pepper, and optional herbs.
  3. Place a portion of the filling in the center of each cabbage leaf and fold the sides over, then roll tightly.
  4. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat and lightly brown the rolls on all sides (optional).
  5. Place the cabbage rolls in a baking dish or pot and pour tomato sauce over them.
  6. Cover and simmer on low heat or bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30-40 minutes until cooked through.
  7. Serve hot as a main dish, optionally garnished with fresh herbs.
