Steak

Steak is a flavorful and satisfying dish made from a cut of beef, typically grilled, pan-seared, or broiled to perfection. It can be cooked to various levels of doneness, from rare to well-done, and is often served with sides like vegetables, potatoes, or sauces. A perfectly cooked steak is tender, juicy, and packed with rich taste.

Ingredient

  • 1 steak (ribeye, sirloin, or preferred cut, about 250-300g)
  • 1-2 tablespoons cooking oil or butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Optional: garlic cloves, rosemary, or thyme for flavoring

Steps

  1. Bring the steak to room temperature and pat it dry with a paper towel.
  2. Season both sides generously with salt and black pepper.
  3. Heat oil or butter in a pan or grill over medium-high heat.
  4. Optional: add garlic cloves and herbs to the pan for extra flavor.
  5. Cook the steak for 3-5 minutes on each side, depending on thickness and desired doneness.
  6. Remove the steak from the pan and let it rest for 5 minutes to retain juices.
  7. Slice against the grain and serve hot with your favorite sides or sauce.
