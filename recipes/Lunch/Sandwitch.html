Sandwich

A sandwich is a quick and versatile meal made by placing fillings such as meat, cheese, vegetables, and spreads between slices of bread. It can be served hot or cold and customized to suit any taste, making it a popular choice for breakfast, lunch, or a light dinner. Sandwiches are easy to prepare and perfect for on-the-go meals.

Ingredient

  • 2 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or preferred type)
  • 2-3 slices of deli meat (chicken, turkey, ham, or preferred)
  • 1-2 slices of cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Cucumber slices (optional)
  • Mayonnaise, mustard, or preferred spread
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Lay out the slices of bread on a clean surface.
  2. Spread mayonnaise, mustard, or your preferred spread on one or both slices of bread.
  3. Layer lettuce leaves, tomato slices, cucumber slices, and deli meat on one slice.
  4. Add slices of cheese on top of the fillings.
  5. Season with salt and pepper if desired.
  6. Place the second slice of bread on top to complete the sandwich.
  7. Cut the sandwich diagonally or in half, if preferred, and serve immediately.
