Salad

Salads are fresh, healthy, and versatile dishes made with a variety of vegetables, fruits, and optional proteins. They can be served as a side or a main course and are perfect for a light, refreshing meal. With endless combinations of ingredients and dressings, salads are a great way to enjoy nutritious food with vibrant flavors.

Ingredient

  • 2 cups mixed lettuce leaves
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup olives (optional)
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese or any preferred cheese (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice or vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and dry all the vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Chop or slice the lettuce, tomato, cucumber, and onion as desired.
  3. Combine all vegetables in a large bowl.
  4. Add shredded carrots, olives, and cheese if using.
  5. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice (or vinegar) over the salad.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Toss gently to mix all ingredients evenly.
  8. Serve immediately as a side or main dish.
