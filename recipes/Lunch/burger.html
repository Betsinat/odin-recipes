Burger

Burgers are a classic and satisfying dish, made with a juicy, seasoned patty usually placed inside a soft bun along with fresh vegetables, cheese, and sauces. They are versatile, allowing endless variations from simple beef patties to chicken, veggie, or gourmet styles. A burger is not only filling but also a favorite comfort food enjoyed around the world.

Ingredient

  • 2 burger buns
  • 250g ground beef (or chicken/veggie alternative)
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil or butter
  • 1 slice of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • Onion slices (optional)
  • Ketchup or mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Season the ground beef with salt and pepper, then shape it into a patty.
  2. Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Cook the patty on both sides until browned and cooked through.
  4. Place a slice of cheese on top of the patty and let it melt (optional).
  5. Toast the burger buns lightly in the same pan.
  6. Assemble the burger by placing the patty on the bottom bun, then adding lettuce, tomato, and onion slices.
  7. Spread ketchup or mayonnaise on the top bun and place it over the fillings.
  8. Serve hot with fries, salad, or your favorite side.
