Fried egg

A fried egg is a quick and delicious way to enjoy eggs, perfect for breakfast or as a topping for rice, toast, or burgers. With its crispy edges and soft, runny yolk (or fully cooked if preferred), a fried egg adds flavor and texture to any meal. Itâ€™s simple to prepare yet satisfying every time.

Ingrident

  • 1 or 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil or butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a non-stick pan on medium heat and add the oil or butter.
  2. Crack the egg gently into the pan.
  3. Let the egg cook without stirring until the whites are set.
  4. Sprinkle salt and pepper on top.
  5. For sunny-side up, cook until the whites are done and the yolk is still runny.
  6. For over-easy, flip the egg gently and cook for 30 seconds more.
  7. Remove carefully and serve hot with bread, rice, or any dish of your choice.
